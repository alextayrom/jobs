Road Map

14 February
OIGGIO Game Launch
The long-awaited day when OIGGIO opens its doors for the first players. This means that all systems and game mechanics, which the team has worked on for many months, are finally ready for use.
19 February
Global Contest Start
Preparation and launch of a balanced gaming contest, in which all game users can participate to compete for valuable prizes.
21 February
Bounty Program
A special bounty program to encourage active OIGGIO players for their gaming achievements and actions that are beneficial for its development.
24 February
Mobile Application
Publication of the official mobile application of OIGGIO's incremental economic strategy for the Android platform.
5 March
Messenger
The first stage in the development of social relationships within OIGGIO — a system of personal messages for players to communicate with each other and important system notifications.
12 March
Level & Boosters System
Implementation of a full-fledged level and experience system, which will subsequently become the foundation for further updates of gameplay mechanics based on it. Introduction of boosters — special items capable of boost your earning.
12 April
Leaderboard
Introduction to the «Leaderboard» feature, a special mechanism that can positively affect the income of the most active players in OIGGIO for various types of activities.
15 June
Dragon Hunt
Launch of the first large-scale event in the OIGGIO incremental economic game. An excellent opportunity to gain fame and earn valuable gifts and prizes.
Soon
Second Game Zone
An alternative game zone with special gameplay. In it, you can level up heroes to perform special contracts and assignments to earn gems or cryptocurrency.
Soon
Knowledge Base
Publication of a publicly accessible knowledge base, where in a simple format every player or ordinary site visitor could learn more about the variety of game mechanics.
Soon
Account Personalization
Comprehensive update dedicated to personalizing your account: the ability to set animated avatars, profile backgrounds, and many other useful features.
Soon
Affiliate Update
Modernization of OIGGIO's affiliate program. A significant overhaul and improvement of its functionality, which will be available to all game partners.
