For the OIGGIO development team and its first players, a significant day has arrived, as today is the official birth day and the beginning of our history count. In this post, we will tell you about the unique mechanics of the project and the process of its creation, as well as briefly about our plans for the future...

Game concept and development stage

The idea of creating not just a game, but a full-fledged service for users to have a good time and at the same time have the opportunity to earn real money appeared for the team that eventually created OIGGIO a very long time ago. However, it was only possible to start implementing their idea in the summer of 2022.

At the stage of developing the concept, it was decided to identify the main principles, which will be the basis of the game during its development and will give direction to its development after its launch:

  1. Active gameplay inspired by incremental games
  2. Social interaction between players (communication, rankings, contests)
  3. Availability from any device, be it a computer, tablet or smartphone
  4. Constant updates both to the ecosystem and the gameplay

When all ideas and principles were thought through, the team set off on an intensive development process, which took more than six months and brought many emotions.

Gameplay

Skipping some creative details and a few intermediate versions, the final gameplay of the future game became reminiscent of an endless interactive battle with colorful but scary monsters, drawn by our designers.

The OIGGIO gameplay offers the player to immerse themselves in a certain fantasy world where a disaster has occurred, about which so far almost no one and nothing is known. Somewhere on the outskirts of this world, a portal has opened with incoming hordes of monsters.

Although the enemies turned out to be extremely weak and they are unable to cause any serious harm, they still look very frightening. And most importantly, by defeating them, you can get valuable gems that the player can exchange for real money.

Economic system

A key feature of any incremental game is large numbers: the bigger the better. The numbers will grow proportionally to your gaming achievements.

You may encounter numbers in the format: 1K, 33M, 699T and so on...For each battle with an opponent, the player will be credited with the OIGGIO in-game currency — gems every second. You can exchange the earned gems for real money and withdraw it to your bank card or electronic wallet. The gems exchange rate is fixed and is $1 = 1M

The main character of the game doesn't want to get his hands dirty and can't fight. Instead, a real army of mercenaries will fight for you, the size and profit of which will directly depend on your actions.

To increase the profit from battles, you need to constantly replenish your army by recruit more and more mercenaries. As a rule, the more expensive the mercenary, the more gems he earns per unit of time.

OIGGIO economy is specifically designed so that any player can play without any investments and at the same time have the opportunity to earn real money.

Cycle system

It's important to understand that cycles have no effect and will not affect your income — it's a game condition, the potential of which will be revealed a little later.The unique feature of the game process is the cycle system, which at the time of the game's release only affects the characteristics of the monsters.

As soon as you defeat the last and strongest opponent, the progress starts again: your next opponent is again the weakest monster. Upon transition to each new cycle, the health of all monsters and the reward for defeating them will increase by 1%.

As the game develops, we plan to work on the cycle system, which will in some way become the platform for introducing a series of future updates.

Roadmap

Despite the fact that the project has just started, around ten major updates are already planned, affecting both the game ecosystem and the gameplay. You can see a brief description of the future updates on the corresponding page. All major updates will be accompanied by a longread similar to this one.

Already in the first month

  • Launch of the first global contest for players
  • A rewards system for in-game achievements
  • OIGGIO mobile app for Android systems
  • Gameplay system of Levels & Boosters
  • Private messaging system
  • Modifiers & Leaders system update
  • New mercenary «Fire Mage»

By combining all the advantages of incremental games and a real money-based economy, we managed to get what we wanted — an exciting game with the opportunity to earn. We are waiting for you among the first users of the economic strategy OIGGIO.
