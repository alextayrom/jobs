Dear players! We are pleased to present to you the global update «Levels & Boosters», which will significantly diversify the gameplay of OIGGIO and add several new mechanics. In this post, we will tell you about levels and yield modifiers, as well as many other changes that have appeared in the game with this update.

Leveling system

The key innovation in this update is the implementation of the leveling system in the gameplay. Levels will become a platform based on which many other innovations will be made in the future. To level up, you need to earn experience by performing various actions, such as:

  • 25% of the income from defeating monsters
  • 50% of the income from completing tasks in the «Quests» page
  • 75% of the income from viewing links in «Website Surfing»
  • 100% of the income from affiliate program

Simply depositing funds will not allow you to level up. Our idea behind the leveling system is to motivate different types of players to take different actions.

Earning experience

Experience is earned for actions related to your earnings in OIGGIO. For example, in the game zone, when collecting a profit of 100K, according to the leveling system's terms, you will automatically receive 25K — exactly 25% of your earnings.

For each subsequent level, more experience is required than for the previous one. We deliberately designed our leveling system so that the first 50 levels could be achieved much easier than any subsequent ones.

For the convenience of level and experience calculation, a special calculator was created, which you can open in the information window of the leveling system Levels → Calculator.

Level rewards

Currently, excluding boosters, which we will talk about in this post a little later, three rewards are available for each level. To receive any reward, you need to reach a certain level, and as the game progresses, the number of rewards will be increased.

  • 25 Level: free message sending in «Messenger»
  • 50 Level: +20% income from website viewing in «Website Surfing»
  • 100 Level: advanced privacy settings

At the time of introducing this update, you can get boosters by reaching a certain level or by participating in contests and promotions on our social networks. More boosters will be available after adding the update «Account Personalization» to the game.

When a booster is activated, your damage per second indicator will increase depending on the strength of the booster used, and as a result, your earnings and experience earning speed will also increase.

Boosters are very difficult to obtain, and their number is limited. That's why we expect that boosters will significantly strengthen the strategic component of OIGGIO, and players will start waiting for the most suitable moment to use them.

Modifiers

The modifiers control panel appears in the game zone from level 10. This is done so as not to overload new players with extra information immediately after completing the tutorial.Together with the introduction of the leveling system in the game, we are pleased to announce the appearance of the «Modifiers» system for you. This system will start working fully after the update «Modifiers & Leaders» is added to the game, but now you have access to «Boosters», which can also change your earnings and game characteristics.

For your convenience, we have created a special panel in the game zone for activating boosters and viewing the list of modifiers. This panel will appear as soon as you reach level 10.

Other changes

To complement the leveling system, we have made many other minor changes that you must know about:

  • In addition to introducing a reward for achieving level 50, which increases your earnings from «Website Surfing» by 20%, we have decided to add an additional progressive bonus scale for viewing a certain number of links.
  • Starting from account level 100, you can hide your position in the leaderboard on the statistics page. You can do this in your profile settings.
  • In the profitability calculator, you can now not only calculate your earnings for an hour, day, week, month, year, but also all the experience earned during these time intervals.
  • After completing a viewing in the «Website Surfing», you can now see the amount of experience you received for viewing.
  • The level of each OIGGIO player is now indicated in their profile and on the account page.

Changelog as of March 12th

  • The game site has been fully localized into Brazilian Portuguese.
  • Section Game Rules 3.4 has been supplemented with a prohibition on the sale and purchase of accounts.
  • In «Quests», the history of completing various tasks has been added.
  • A notification of success or failure during quest moderation by the administration has been added.
  • A bug that caused a referral in your list of referrals to be duplicated multiple times has been fixed.
  • Incorrect display of the DPS graph in the profile has been fixed.
  • Incorrect display of the referral sources graph has been fixed.

Thank you for reading this post. We have briefly told you about the main functions, innovations, and new mechanics of the first major update in OIGGIO. Thank you for staying with us; there are many more massive updates ahead of you.
