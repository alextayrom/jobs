We continue to move along the roadmap and work on the development of the social aspect in the game. This time OIGGIO has introduced a «Messenger» — a special application that allows you to communicate with other players directly in game.

«Messenger»

Now every OIGGIO player can send a personal message to any other player. This significantly simplifies social interaction within the game, you can ask your referrer something, write to a friend, or just start a conversation with one of the players.

At the moment, we have made a simplified version of the messenger, and soon we will add the ability to attach attachments, verification for well-known players, an advanced system for managing contacts and notifications. However, even the basic version is great for simple dialogs, try writing to your friend right now.

How to write a message?

The easiest, but not the only way, is to open the profile of any player and click on the «Message» button. If you already know the login of the person you want to send a message to, just click on the «New Dialogue» button right on the messenger page.

Payment for a message

In the update «Level & Boosters System», you will be able to disable payment as a reward for reaching a certain level, but we will tell you about it later.Unfortunately, in order to combat spam, we are forced to introduce a fee for communication. It will be charged only from the sender and only for the very first message in the dialogue. Currently, the cost of sending the first message is 1000 or $0.001.

Player blocking

A user with whom you do not want to continue communicating for any reason can be blocked in the dialogue menu. Once you do this, the player will not be able to send you messages. This will last until you unblock them yourself.

If blocking is not enough, you can always complain about the user. Just like blocking, this is done through the dialogue menu. The OIGGIO administration will consider the complaint, and if it is confirmed, the player may be restricted from communicating through the «Messenger».

Changelog as of March 5th

  • Legal information has been published on the «Help» and «Rules» pages.
  • The level of optimization for the most demanding pages in the mobile version of the site and the OIGGIO mobile application has been significantly increased.
  • A window with a detailed description of the in-game currency has been added. To open it, simply hover over or click on your balance.
  • Now advertisers can edit the link title in surfing, even if it is in a launched state, during editing.
  • An error in surfing has been fixed, which prevented the content from being seen or read when viewing the site without a background.
  • A problem that made it impossible to determine the exact balance on mobile devices has been fixed.

We hope you enjoyed today's update. There are still many other wonderful innovations ahead, both in the social aspect and the gameplay of OIGGIO. Stay with us and follow us on social media so as not to miss them.
