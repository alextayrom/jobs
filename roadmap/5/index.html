Dear players! In this post, we will introduce you to a new mercenary — «Fire Mage», answer some questions, explain why some updates are being postponed to the next roadmap, and also share our future plans with you.

🔥 «Fire Mage»

As planned, we are opening up the possibility of hiring the 11th mercenary, he is available for purchase right now. Many have already guessed that the last two mercenaries will cost more than the previous ones, and someone even accurately predicted its profitability or release date.

This is what the brief information about the new mercenary looks like. The full information is already available for study, you can find it directly in the game.

We see the introduction of new mercenaries as a tool to positively influence the stable and smooth growth of OIGGIO according to our plans. That is why the release date and characteristics of the last mercenary will be a secret until its release.

Updates postponement

Despite the already rapid pace of releasing new updates, the most vigilant players may have noticed that we have deviated significantly from our roadmap. The month is already coming to an end, but three updates have not yet been published.

Why did this happen?

The administration, together with the development team, worked on the roadmap back in January, with the expectation that the game launch would take place on February 4th. However, due to technical reasons, we were only able to officially launch OIGGIO on February 14th.

Despite the delayed launch date, we decided not to deviate from the planned roadmap, although this greatly increased the workload on the development team, which did happen. Nevertheless, we want to assure you that all three updates are currently ready.

Reason for the postponement

The «Private Messages» update was released on the day of this announcement, but due to the fact that we needed to release three news items within two days, we refrained from adding another one to them.

«Private Messages» will be added to OIGGIO approximately on March 2-3.At the moment, the administration is completely satisfied with the pace of the game's development according to all statistical indicators. At some point, it became clear that artificially making changes to the current pace of development ahead of time is not the best idea.

Therefore, the decision was made to postpone the updates for «Level & Boosters System» and «Modifiers & Leaders» to the spring roadmap, as they could affect the smooth and steady growth of OIGGIO if we added them at an early stage.

Spring roadmap

With the coming of spring, specifically at 00:00 UTC+1, we will publish the spring roadmap. It will include numerous amazing updates that we have planned, as well as those updates that were decided not to be released in February. Don't miss this event and be sure to read our next post, it will be available very soon.

March in OIGGIO will be very eventful in interesting updates, and closer to summer we will have an alternative gaming zone, in addition to the one where you need to buy mercenaries and deal with monsters, but we will tell you more about this later. 😉

We hope we have explained to you why some updates were decided to be postponed. Already in March, all three postponed updates will be published.
