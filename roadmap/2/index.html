The first update in the incremental strategy game OIGGIO, according to our roadmap, will be the addition of a global contest. All players, without exception, will be able to compete for valuable cash prizes. In this post, we will tell you what a global contest is and how to participate and win.

How does the global contest work?

It's very simple: on the contest page, there is a leaderboard table based on points, and by earning them, you advance on the leaderboard. The person who is in the top twenty at the end of the contest will receive a prize according to their position on the leaderboard.

  1. The global contest is the main and most prestigious contest in OIGGIO, and most importantly, it is currently the only one in which you can participate. In the future, new types of activities and contests will definitely appear in the game.
  2. All OIGGIO players participate in the contest automatically and can track their current number of points directly on the global contest page.
  3. After the end of the contest, all prizes will be instantly credited to the account balance and will be immediately available for payout.

How to earn points?

Points are automatically added for certain actions. At the time of the launch of the first global contest, the points system looks like this:

  • 1 point for every $ of your deposit
  • 0.25 points for every $ of your referral's deposit
  • 0.15 points for every $ payout by you
  • 0.05 points for viewing a link in surfing

Test period

The first contest is being held faster than usual, including in order to determine the final prize pool and basic terms of the contest.

We want to gather maximum feedback from players regarding our idea of a global contest.Initially, it was planned that the global contest would be held monthly and its prize pool would be 2.5 billion, but in the end it was decided to hold the first global contest in a more compressed timeframe. This is necessary in order to calibrate and balance the points system for future contests, since we want to achieve equal chances of winning for investors and partners simultaneously.

All subsequent contests will start and end on the 1st of each month, and their prize pool will significantly increase, unlike the first trial contest.

  • The first contest starts: February 19 at 12:00 UTC+1
  • The first contest ends: March 1 at 20:00 UTC+1

Social value

One of the most important development directions for OIGGIO is social. Already, players can view the game profiles of other players, and very soon we will have our own internal messenger, thanks to which players will be able to communicate and receive important system notifications right inside the game.

Contests are part of the foundation of the game's social life. Players who were able to take prize places in the contest will be featured on our social networks and chats, and unique badges will be placed next to their avatar in their personal profile.

Changelog as of February 19

Since the launch of OIGGIO, there have been many small and larger changes. From now on, each news post will include a list of changes made by the development team since the publication of the previous post.

  • Added the OIGGIO game page to the review aggregators TrustPilot and WOT. We would be grateful if you leave a review about us there.
  • Added the ability to update the captcha image when viewing surfing. To do this, simply click on the image itself.
  • Added new methods of purchasing gems: AlfaBank SBER Tinkoff SBP
  • Redesigned payment methods for purchases in RUB, updated commission for top-ups with the new or updated methods is now 0%.
  • Added an alternative way to advance to the next stage of training due to problems with passing training on some Android devices.
  • Fixed an issue with referral income not being credited on the first day of the game launch. All uncredited revenue from the affiliate program has been successfully credited.
  • Fixed an issue with inaccurate display of the referral income amount in the list of referrals.
  • Fixed the issue with the absence of graphs in the personal profile.
  • Fixed the issue with the formation and rounding of decimal amounts.
  • Added the ability to create a login using an underscore.

Today we have fully begun our movement along the roadmap. In a few days, we will finish working on adding «Quests» — another way to earn gems. The next post will be about what they are and how to earn on them. Stay tuned for updates.
